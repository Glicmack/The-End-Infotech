WordPress vs Custom Web Development

Many clients wonder whether to use a website builder, WordPress, or a custom-built system.
When done right, WordPress is a solid, cost-effective foundation; we build it from scratch without bloated plugins or shortcuts, so it stays clean and scalable. If your project requires advanced logic, deep integrations, or headless architecture, custom development gives you full control — but it naturally takes more resources.
At the end of the day, don’t reinvent the wheel: if WordPress covers your goals, keep it smart and simple; if your growth plans demand more, custom is the right move.
